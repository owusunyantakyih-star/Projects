CYBERSAFE EXPO '24

Stay Aware, Stay Safe

EVENT DETAILS

Date:

2024-11-30

Time:

09:00 to 13:00

Venue:
George Brown College, Casa Loma Campus, Main Auditorium
Purpose:

The Cyber Expo aims to empower individuals of all ages with the knowledge and skills needed to protect themselves against cyber threats. By providing comprehensive education on cybersecurity practices, we aspire to create a safer online environment for everyone. Through engaging workshops, expert-led discussions, and interactive activities, attendees will learn how to identify and respond to cyber attacks, ensuring they can navigate the digital world with confidence and security.
